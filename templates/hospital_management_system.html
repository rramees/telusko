Streamlining Door Manufacturing Processes

Our client, a prominent door manufacturing company in South India, faced significant challenges in managing orders from a network of agents. The existing system relied on agents submitting order details through WhatsApp and written forms, resulting in errors such as unclear images, incomplete information, and a lack of real-time product availability updates. This led to delays, missed promises on delivery dates, and increased manufacturing costs due to errors and inefficiencies.

The Problem:

  • Unclear Image Submissions: Agents often sent unclear or inadequate images of the requested doors, leading to errors in product identification and manufacturing.
  • Incomplete Information: Agents lacked a comprehensive understanding of product details, including measurements and real-time availability at the main manufacturing facility.
  • Customer Promise Delays: Inaccurate information and delays in availability updates caused disruptions in the manufacturing process, leading to unmet customer delivery promises.

The Solution:

To address these challenges, we developed a centralized platform - a mobile application accessible to all agents. This application aimed to streamline the order submission process and provide agents with clear information on products.

Key Features of the Application:

  • Detailed Image Descriptions: Agents could view detailed images and descriptions of the doors, ensuring accurate identification and eliminating errors in product selection.
  • Clear Measurement Submission: The application facilitated clear and standardized submission of door measurements, reducing ambiguity and errors in manufacturing.
  • Real-time Availability Status: Agents could check the real-time availability of selected items at the main manufacturing facility, enabling them to make informed promises to customers.

Results and Impact:

  • Improved Manufacturing Speed: The centralized platform allowed the main manufacturing base to initiate production rapidly, as clear and accurate details were provided by the agents.
  • Cost Savings: Reduction in errors and streamlined processes led to significant cost savings in manufacturing, as materials were utilized efficiently, and rework due to errors was minimized.
  • Enhanced Customer Satisfaction: The improved accuracy in order details and adherence to promised delivery dates resulted in higher customer satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Efficiency in Order Processing: The application generated ready-to-use PDF formats containing all necessary calculations and details, reducing the workload on the manufacturing team. This transition from a multi-person order management system to a single-point entry increased efficiency.

Conclusion:

The implementation of a centralized platform for order submission not only addressed the existing challenges faced by our client but also brought about substantial improvements in efficiency, cost savings, and customer satisfaction. The streamlined processes facilitated by the application have positioned our client as an industry leader, capable of meeting customer demands with precision and reliability.
